A Tang dynasty stele (partial). October 2001, China.

Initially established in AD 1087 when some precious stone steles were moved here for safe keeping, the Forest of Stone Steles Museum in Xi'an holds a large collection of the earliest stone steles of different periods, from the Han Dynasty to the Qing Dynasty. 

照片号SX001。西安碑林唐碑局部。2001年10月。

始建于1087年的西安碑林拥有大量从汉朝到清朝的珍贵石刻。
