Tibetan girl. China, May 2002. Photo ID: Tibet005. 

This photo was shot when I was in the half way of surrounding the holy Mt. Kailash(6714m) . I met her in a temple. This no-name temple located on the mountain slope might be the highest temple of the world with estimate altitude of 5500m. I was half dead because of the high altitude, I just could not forget her eyes. 

照片号Tibet005。这张照片是我在海拔6714米的圣山冈仁波齐转山时拍摄的。我在山腰的一个寺庙里遇见了这个藏族女孩。这个寺庙估计海拔5500米，可能是世界海拔最高的寺庙。高海拔把我折腾得半死，但这个女孩的眼光实在让我难以忘怀。2002年5月。西藏。
