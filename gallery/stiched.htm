Great night view of Singapore China town, streets of old colonial houses with modern buildings in the background. This picture was shot in January 2005,  just few days before the celebration of Chinese new year. People were shopping like crazy for Chinese new year goodies.

照片号SIN003。

新加坡牛车水（唐人街），2005年1月。古老的红色殖民地时代的房子和现代化的摩天大楼交相辉映。拍摄时间正是农历新年之前，人们抢着来这里购买年货。
