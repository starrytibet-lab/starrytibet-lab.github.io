Stock Photography and Commercial Licensing

　

All images in my gallery can be licensed for any use, including advertising, corporate, promotional, editorial illustration in magazines and books, web, media, presentations, decor, and retail products.

　

Delivery. Images are available as high resolution digital files, up to 3000x5000 pixels (11x17 inches at 300dpi) which can generally be made available by email or FTP download. Unless specified otherwise, images will be transmitted in Adobe RGB, saved as high quality Jpeg.

　

Pricing. You will find my pricing extremely fair and competitive and matches the price of any other stock photography agency. However, no transaction will be conducted below the minimal licensing fee of US$100.

Quotes. For a quote, provide as much detail as you can. This is important because if I do not have enough information, I would assume that you require more general rights, resulting in a higher quote. Please email me with the following information:

  • Name/Organization

  • Contact phone number/e-mail address

  • End use of piece/Name of client

  • Usage type/Size/Placement

  • Circulation/Distribution/Duration

  • Requested rights/Other uses

Thank you and please do not hesitate to contact me by email or phone.
