周丽蓉和新婚老公赵峰一起经营着一家服装店。他们还有另外一处电脑店。店面在上海浦东川沙镇，门面虽然不大但这两个店子平均一个月有七八千元收入。赵峰是土生土长的上海本地人，丽蓉是四川泸州人，她现在最大的希望就是继续扩大生意，等将来有钱了就能在上海市区再开店，也许回泸州再开个店，然后两口子还可以出门旅游。下面这个图就是他们的服装店，名叫10。

Photo ID: Shanghai723E.

Ms Zhou li-rong, recently married, runs a costume shop together with her husband，Mr ZhaoFeng. They have another computer shop as well, both located at Chuan-sha, the town of Shanghai Pudong. The shops are small but the average monthly profit is around RMB7,000. Mr Zhao Feng was born and grown up locally, Li-rong was born in Lu-zhou of Sichuan province. They wish the business can grow continually, so in the future they can afford to run a shop in downtown shanghai, and maybe another one in Lu-zhou, by then they shall be able to travel extensively.
