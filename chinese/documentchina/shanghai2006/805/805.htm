上海图文日记2006

2006年8月5日

我从今天开始写英文，以后有时间我会尽快翻译成中文。中文读者请原谅，英文好的朋友请多指正。感谢。

------------------------------------------------------

5 Aug 2006

I am a wedding photographer the whole day. what else can i say? By all means i am not good at wedding, but I have promised my friends on the train to Shanghai. I should learn more the art of turning people down.

Yesterday I spent 5 hours in the Oriental Pearl Tower (5-10pm). I was there shooting like crazy and came out with over 300 images. This tower is no doubt the greatest place to shoot the night in shanghai, the RMB85 entrance charge is worth every penny.

At the check point I had a problem bringing in my SONY HC1E video camera. The security guy said it looks professional. What the stupid security guy didn't know is that i have a much more pro camera with me, the Canon EOS 1ds Mark II. I complained to the manager and the problem solved.

I would like to thank Lao Liu for a great Sichuan cuisine treat last night at a small restaurant near Lianhua subway station. Lao Liu is an experienced Shanghainese photographer who had provided me with lots of info via MSN on taking photo inside the tower. Thank you.
