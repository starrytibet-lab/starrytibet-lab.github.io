上海图文日记2006

2006年8月7日

今天身体不太好， 下午四点才出门，苏州河边走了走。天黑在路边一个小店吃了碗面就回来了。

　

7 Aug 2006

Not feeling very well today, i stayed home till 4pm. Walked along the Suzhou creek for a while, had a quick dinner at a roadside noodle stall.
