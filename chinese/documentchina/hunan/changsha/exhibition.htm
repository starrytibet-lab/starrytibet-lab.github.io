2003年冬天在湖南省长沙岳麓山脚下拍摄的麓山书院墙壁。麓山书院始建于宋朝，这个有1000多年历史的书院是中国最古老的书院之一，但历史上多次毁于战火，现在我们看到的绝大部分书院建筑是1990年重建的。

Photo ID: CS002. The wall and tree of Yuelu Academy, winter of 2003, Changsha, Hunan, China. Located on the foot of Yuelu mountain, dated more than 1000 years back in the Song dynasty, the Yuelu academy is one of the oldest in China. Most of its old buildings were repeatly destroyed and then rebuilt. This wall is part of the recent 1990 rebuilding.
