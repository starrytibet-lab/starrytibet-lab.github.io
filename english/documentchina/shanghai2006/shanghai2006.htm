                                                                                                                                         Summer of 2006 - A Photographer's Shanghai Diary 

 David Yang, Aug 2006

A sunny afternoon, 16 July 2006. The first day in Shanghai I was at the famous People's Square. All in a sudden  a  shabby dressed little girl run down to me. "Give me some money, give me some money", clearly she was speaking in English. I was astonished. Beggars are everywhere in China but this was my first encounter with a English speaking Chinese beggar. Is it because with a big camera on hand, I looked a little like a tourist from the West?...read more
