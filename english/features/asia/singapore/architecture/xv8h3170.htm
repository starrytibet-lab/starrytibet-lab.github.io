Photo ID: SIN0149. Gateway towers, 37-storey high, one of the landmark office buildings of Singapore. Design by I.M Pei. It's a good photographic object, but i clashed with the building securities, they did not event allow me to shoot outside the building. I did it. Here they are. Jan 2006, Canon 1ds mark II camera, EF28-135IS lens.

照片号SIN0149。新加坡杰门（Gateway）办公大楼，楼高37层，是新加坡的标志性办公楼之一，由著名的贝聿铭同志设计。这是一个很好的拍摄对象，但是我和大楼的保安差点打了起来，他们甚至不准我在建筑的四周拍摄。不过最后他们还是没干过我。此图为证。 2006年元月，佳能1ds mark II照相机，EF28-135IS 镜头。
