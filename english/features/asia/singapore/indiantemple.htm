Singapore Hindu Temples

 There are 27 images in this page, mainly from the Sri Mariamman Temple. Pictures of other Hindu temples also included. Please click on the below thumbnails.

Out of dozens of Hindu temples, the most well-known is the Sri Mariamman. Originally built in 1827, it is also the oldest Hindu temple in Singapore.  Interestingly It is located in the heart of Singapore Chinatown and tightly surrounded by many southeastern Asia style Chinese houses. See below picture for the location of Sri Mariamman Temple. This picture was shoot from the 24th floor of HDB apartment block 335B in January 2005, just a few days before the Chinese new year celebration, people were crazy shopping for new year goodies.

Night view of Singapore China town. Photo ID: SIN003

新加坡牛车水（唐人街）夜景。图片号：SIN003

I have been a frequent visitor to Sri Mariamman Temple ever since I first picked up my camera in 2001, and particularly so during my doing of Block 335B project in 2005 and 2006. If you are interested in the everyday life of Singaporean, please click here  to see my on-going Block 335B documentary photography project.

Related thread: the Hinduism Taipusum Festival images. Please take note the exotica of the Taipusum requires some courage to appreciate.

　

新 加 坡 的 印 度 庙

此印度庙(兴都庙）专辑共有27张图片，包括马里安曼兴都庙和其他寺庙。请点击下面的小图。在新加坡的几十个印度庙里，最著名最古老的是始建于1827年的马里安曼兴都庙（Sri Mariamman）。有趣的是， 这座印度庙位于牛车水唐人街，四周都被南洋风格的中式排屋包围着。从上图您可以看到马里安曼兴都庙在牛车水的位置。这张照片是从牛车水335B座组屋的24楼拍摄的，时间为2005年元月，农历新年之前，人们抢着来这里购买年货。

从2001年开始学摄影算起，我来过（路过）马里安曼兴都庙少说也有十多次。2005年和06年间我一直在拍摄大牌335B这个项目，此庙就在不远处。如果您对新加坡人的日常生活感兴趣的话请点击 这里 看看我不断更新中的大牌335B纪实摄影项目。

推荐相关阅读：印度教 大宝森节专辑。请注意新加坡大宝森节的图片需要一点勇气来看。
