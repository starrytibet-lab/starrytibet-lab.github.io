Photo ID: SIN0273. CBD Singapore. Sigma 17-35mm lens @ 20mm. Canon 1ds Mark II camera on tripod.  Dec 28, 2005.

Standing on the edge of the 46th floor (un-obscured, see the right bottom of the picture) , One Raffles Place, an in-construction office tower. The securities just did not allow me to go in. I spent 2 hours scouting around the construction site and finally did it. It was raining and the wind was blowing hard, that was really a scaring suicidal experience. You wouldn't be able to see this picture if i took one step forward. Lucky me.
