Photo ID: SIN003. From the  24th floor corridor of block 335B.  Night view of Singapore China town, streets of colonial era houses with modern buildings in the background. This picture was shot in January 2005,  just few days before the celebration of Chinese new year. People were shopping like crazy for Chinese new year goodies.  The following 4 pictures were shot from the same spot.

The widest angle of Canon Pro1 digital camera is 28 mm, which was not wide enough for this view. I took 2 shots and then stitched them together in the PC.

照片号SIN003。大牌335B组屋24楼 上的风景。2005年1月。古老的红色殖民地时代的房子和现代化的摩天大楼交相辉映。拍摄时间正是农历新年之前，人们抢着来这里购买年货。以下四张图片都是在相同的地点拍摄的。佳能PRO1数码相机最大广角只有28MM，不够广，此图是我拍了两张回家在电脑里拼接的。
