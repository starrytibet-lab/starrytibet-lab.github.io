Photography Services

　

Specializing in reportage and travel photography, I am readily available for various commercial photography assignments, except wedding/studio portrait.

Pricing of the assignment depends on both the complexity and logistics of the assignment, as well as on the usage rights required by the client. The guideline price starts from US$300 per day. This price is for the photography service solely, other expenses, e.g. traveling, accommodation, photography assistant (if any), photo finish, etc, will be billed separately.  50% advance payment is required prior to the assignment.

I have abandoned film since mid-2005, my main camera is Canon 1ds Mark II, the best 135 format digital SLR in the world.

If you wish an estimate for a given assignment, please contact me by phone or email.
